iRepair - Professional iPhone Repair Services

At iRepair, we specialize in delivering professional and reliable iPhone repair services to restore your device to perfect working condition. From hardware fixes to software troubleshooting, we handle everything with precision and expertise.

Our Services Include:
  • Screen & Display Repairs: Fix cracked screens, unresponsive touch displays, and display discoloration issues.
  • Battery Replacement: Resolve issues like rapid battery drainage and non-charging iPhones with our premium-quality battery replacements.
  • Water Damage Repair: Advanced techniques to revive water-damaged iPhones while protecting your data and components.
  • Speaker & Microphone Repair: Crystal-clear sound restoration with speaker and microphone repairs or replacements.
  • Camera Repairs: Repair or replace faulty front or rear cameras for sharp, clear photos and videos.
  • Charging Port Repairs: Fix loose or non-functional charging ports to ensure smooth charging and connectivity.
  • Button Repairs: Resolve issues with the power button, volume buttons, or home button.
  • Baseband & Network Repairs: Solve "No Service," "No SIM," or "No Modem" issues, ensuring your iPhone stays connected.
  • Data Recovery: Recover your valuable data even from severely damaged iPhones using advanced board-swapping and recovery techniques.
